About

I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science, and my research centers on advancing Video and Image Learned Compression. For my thesis, I am exploring scalable image compression to combine the strengths of both hand-crafted and learned compression techniques in a single framework. My research spans diverse areas, showcasing a blend of skills in hardware design, optimization, algorithm development, and machine learning. This interdisciplinary approach allows me to apply my knowledge across various domains, solving complex problems and driving innovation.
